Our Heritage

The Story of NIOÉLIA

NIOÉLIA comes from merging "NIO" and "ÉLIA". In the local dialect of Zakynthos, "NIO" means each year's new olive oil, while "ÉLIA" refers to the ancient, blessed tree that offers us its wonderful juice.

The "NIO oil" is an expression that has accompanied us since childhood. When we saw our parents and grandparents, at the beginning of October, prepare for the new olive oil season by making wooden rods and ladders, preparing the crates, sieves, nets, and oilcloths.

We were eager to taste the "NIO" together with freshly baked bread as it flowed from the separator in the olive mill. To feel that characteristic bitterness in the throat—where we now know it comes from the high concentration of phenols.

High Phenolic EVOO

NIOÉLIA is an export-oriented Greek premium extra virgin olive oil of early harvest. Our single estate terroir product is a high phenolic extra virgin olive oil collected on the eastern slopes of Mount Vrachiona at 220m altitude.

100% Koroneiki

Single variety olives solely from our family groves, hand-picked when still green.

Cold Extraction

Daily pressing under cold extraction to preserve superior quality.

Silver Category

Therapeutic Properties

Tasting Profile

Color: Intensely bright green due to high chlorophyll content

Taste: Fruity and intensely bitter with orange aroma

Note: No preservatives or additives. Store in a cool, dark place (max 22°C) for up to 18 months. Delivery conditions should meet the storage conditions mentioned above. Do not refrigerate. Sedimentation is characteristic of extra virgin olive oil.
